Partnerships

We co-develop practical research, training, and tools with universities, institutes, and producer networks. Collaborations prioritize Somali needs, shared SOPs/standards, and open, reusable outputs.

How we collaborate

  • Co-research
  • Training & co-supervision
  • Coordinated field / lab work
  • Methods & data sharing
  • Joint project application & implementation
