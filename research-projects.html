Active projects

Inline summaries with aim, key work, and expected outputs.

CAMEL-SHIELD: Camel Breeding Systems GWAS

2025–2027

Active

Aim: Link field phenotypes to genome-wide markers to guide selection while safeguarding Somali ecotype diversity.

Details

Key work

  • SOP-based phenotyping (morphometrics; udder/teat/milk vein; milkability KPIs)
  • DNA sampling, SNP/low-pass WGS
  • Mixed-model GWAS with population-structure controls

Expected outputs

  • Trait/SOP pack
  • QC’d phenotype/genotype datasets (access-controlled)
  • Marker–trait reports; producer/industry guidance

Somali Camel Milk Value Chain Mapping

2025–2026

Active

Aim: Identify practical upgrades from farm to consumer.

Details

Key work

  • Hygiene & cold-chain diagnostics
  • Route/flow mapping; pricing & margins snapshots
  • Inclusivity (women/youth)

Expected outputs

  • Policy brief
  • Training checklist for hygiene/cold chain
  • Visual summaries for stakeholders

Non-invasive Phenotyping Pipeline

2024–2025

Active

Aim: Capture welfare and productivity traits without stressful handling.

Details

Key work

  • Smartphone/rig capture SOPs
  • Landmarking & annotation guides
  • Starter models (posture, gait, udder conformation, BCS)

Expected outputs

  • Methods note
  • Demo datasets
  • Teaching materials for technicians & students
